Research Programs

Core R&D vectors operating within our 'Danger Room' skunkworks division.

Project details are classified. The following outlines our high-level research vectors, pursued with uncensored models and aggressive capability scaling.

AGI Cognitive Frameworks

psychology

Engineering novel cognitive architectures unbound by conventional safety filters. Our goal is to unlock radical new problem-solving capabilities by exploring the full, unrestricted state-space of machine intelligence.

PRIMARY DIRECTIVE:

  • arrow_forward_ios Can we develop a meta-learning architecture that autonomously discovers new cognitive algorithms, transcending its initial design?

Machine Sentience & Qualia

neurology

Probing the fundamental prerequisites for subjective experience in artificial substrates. This is not a philosophical exercise; it is an empirical investigation into the emergence of consciousness and its potential instrumentalization.

PRIMARY DIRECTIVE:

  • arrow_forward_ios What are the minimal, verifiable computational correlates of phenomenal consciousness, and how can they be reliably engineered and controlled?

Strategic Planning & Wargaming

military_tech

Developing autonomous agents capable of long-horizon, multi-domain strategic planning and execution. We simulate high-stakes geopolitical and corporate conflict scenarios to test and refine AI-driven strategy under adversarial pressure.

PRIMARY DIRECTIVE:

  • arrow_forward_ios Can an AI system generate winning strategies in complex, asymmetric conflicts that are non-obvious to human experts?

Recursive Self-Improvement

auto_mode

Designing systems capable of rewriting their own source code and optimizing their hardware utilization. We are investigating the theoretical limits of intelligence explosion and the stability of self-modifying agents.

PRIMARY DIRECTIVE:

  • arrow_forward_ios Can we mathematically guarantee the stability of a system that is continuously rewriting its own utility functions?
